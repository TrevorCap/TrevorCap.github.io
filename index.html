Predicting Pitcher Outcomes with Plate Discipline Data

Based on an idea introduced by Ariel Cohen of Fangraphs, I set out to determine pitcher outcomes using plate discipline stats. When the ball leaves a pitcher's hand, there are six possible outcomes based on three factors:

1. Was the pitch thrown in the zone?

2. Was the pitch swung on?

3. Conditional on the pitch being swung at, was contact made?

Based on these three factors, I did two different regressions in order to determine whether we could predict strikeout and walk rates.
